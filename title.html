Angel

#Cinema4D   #After Effects

Inspired by George Orwell book "1984", I created an animation for an imaginary movie "Angel" as its title of sequence. "Dystopia" is the main theme in my animation. In a world full of manipulation, lures and cheating. No one can have absolute freedom. With so much negative energy surrounded, how can a man maintain his identity and faith? What is his right direction? Will there be any angel coming to give him an answer?
