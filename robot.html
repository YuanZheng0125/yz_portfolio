Ocean Trash Cleanser

#Cinema 4D  #Photoshop

Inspired by two pieces of work, Ponyo on the Cliff and Nausicaa Of The Valley Of The Wind, from my favorite Japanese artist, Hayao Miyazaki, I want to use my own language of design to show my concern for the ocean pollution. I want to design a new aqua drone that can help clean and recycle underwater garbage.
